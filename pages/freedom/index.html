About

Design for Good is the name of a course I took this past spring. In this course we had to chose an non-profit organization with a social mission, rebrand them, as well as come up with their collateral and a campaign idea for them. The company I chose was Freedom From Fear, an organization based out in New York, started in the 80's to help people deal with Depression and Anxiety.

Final Logo

With this organization, I was beginning from scratch as they don't have much at all to their image. Starting with the logo, I chose to juxtapose a heavy font for "Freedom" with a thinner font for "From Fear" to keep the attention on "Freedom". Along with this, the colors chosen have meaning behind them, gold represents triumph and success, blue to represent tranquility and confience, and little bits of black to represent the anxiety and depression they are fighting against.
