About

Simultaneous Contrast is about placing colors on different background to give the illusion that they are of the same value. I chose to realize this illusion by allowing the user to create it themselves through an interactive experience. I made a package called "Simultaneous Contrast" which comes with an instruction manual, 6 pairs of cards, 4 templates, and a clear case for all of it.

After opening the package it comes with the following:


Case and Template, 6 pairs of swatch cards, and an instruction manual

How it works:
Grabbing the template, the user sticks it into the clear case, then takes matching pair of cards (i.e the two gray cards with As on them.) and puts the card with the light A in the white side of the template and the one with the dark A in the black side. After that, the color on white will darken and the color on dark will lighten, making them look like the same color.
