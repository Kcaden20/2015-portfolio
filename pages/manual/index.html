About

A manual on Silkscreening. It is branded under my Sushi Time Co. Line and is called a cookbook to continue with the food theme. In this manual, I give a list of materials needed for photo emulsion screenprinting, go over how to expose a screen, and talk about the different sort of effects one can get with screenprinting.

Each chapter opening is followed by a section giving advice for prepping and things to think about while printing

Simple line illustrations are used to help explain the process
