About

A mapping of the 3rd floor silkscreen studio. The title "The Forgotten Studio" refers to all of the old screens left in the studio. Abandoned and unreclaimed after they were used. Taking all of these screens, I gave them renewed purpose not only through printing with them again to create something new but also exhibiting the screens themselves to allow people to pick through them and see the culture and story that the 3rd floor studio as had.
