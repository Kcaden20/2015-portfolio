About

A Better World by Design is a student-run conference held every year on Brown/RISD's campus. This past conference, I was the branding coordinator in charge of coming up with the visual representation of the theme which was "WayFinding". The final logo is an abstraction of the college hill area divided into Brown and RISD area.

Final Logo

Initial research into "Wayfinding" included looking at other Wayfinding systems and how they tended to brand themselves as well as the looking at the sort of colors that tend to be used in these systems. My notes include looking at and analyzing systems like "The Pittsburgh Wayfinding System." The red, blue, yellow color scheme was chosen to as they are the most used colors in Wayfinding systems. The font "Apex New" is the font used for A Better World by Design so that ties it back to the brand.
